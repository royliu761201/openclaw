# NSFC Grant Application Guidelines (2025/2026)

## 1. Official Template Source
**CRITICAL**: You MUST download the official proposal template from the [NSFC Grants System](https://grants.nsfc.gov.cn/).
- The template contains a unique **Watermark** (e.g., "NSFC 2025") and validation metadata.
- **DO NOT** use old templates or third-party Word files, as likely to be rejected.

## 2. Key Programs
### Research Fund for International Scientists (RFIS)
- **RFIS-I (Young Scientists)**: PhD within 10 years.
- **RFIS-II (Excellent Scientists)**: Associate Prof level, ~15 years track record.
- **RFIS-III (Senior Scientists)**: Full Prof level.
- **Submission**: English Proposal + Chinese Abstract.

### General Program (面上项目)
- **Language**: Chinese.
- **Focus**: Innovative scientific research.
- **Budget**: Direct costs ~500k-600k RMB.

## 3. Writing Structure (General)
1.  **立项依据 (Scientific Basis)**:
    - **Scientific Problem**: What is the core question?
    - **State of Art**: Review recent literature (last 3-5 years).
    - **Gap**: What is missing? Why is it urgent?
2.  **研究内容 (Research Content)**:
    - 3-4 specific research targets.
    - **Key Scientific Question** (关键科学问题): Must be singular and deep.
3.  **研究方案 (Methodology)**:
    - Technical route (Flowchart).
    - Feasibility analysis (Risk mitigation).
4.  **特色与创新 (Innovation)**:
    - 1-2 points max. Don't overclaim.

## 4. Updates for 2025
- **Budget**: Strict adherence to "Budget Preparation Requirements". Direct/Indirect cost splits.
- **Talent Programs**: "Basic Science Center" renamed "Excellent Research Group".

## 5. Agent Instructions
When the `GrantWriter` agent drafts a proposal, it generates the **Content** (Text/LaTeX). You must copy-paste this content into the **Official Word Template** downloaded from the system.
